个人简介 About Me

我是北京大学教育学院教育技术系2024级硕士研究生。本科毕业于华东师范大学，获教育技术学和计算机科学与技术双学士学位。我的研究兴趣主要集中在学习分析、人工智能教育应用、教育数据挖掘和个性化学习系统等领域。我致力于运用数据科学和人工智能技术改进教育实践，提升学习效果。

I am a Master's student (Class of 2024) in the Department of Educational Technology at the Graduate School of Education, Peking University. I received my dual Bachelor's degrees in Educational Technology and Computer Science and Technology from East China Normal University (2020-2024). My research interests focus on Learning Analytics, Artificial Intelligence in Education (AIED), Educational Data Mining, and Personalized Learning Systems. I am committed to applying data science and artificial intelligence technologies to improve educational practices and enhance learning outcomes.

学术成果 Publications

自我调节学习中的即时测验：基于认知负荷理论的实证研究 Immediate quizzing in self-regulated learning: An empirical study based on cognitive load theory 第一作者 First Author

李子健, 樊一舟, 等

Zijian Li, Yizhou Fan, et al.

British Journal of Educational Technology (BJET), 2024 (已接收) British Journal of Educational Technology (BJET), 2024 (Accepted)

本研究基于认知负荷理论，探讨了即时测验在自我调节学习中的作用。通过实证研究，我们发现即时测验能够有效降低学习者的认知负荷，提高学习效率。研究结果对在线教育平台的设计和实施具有重要的指导意义。

This study investigates the role of immediate quizzing in self-regulated learning based on cognitive load theory. Through empirical research, we found that immediate quizzing can effectively reduce learners' cognitive load and improve learning efficiency. The results have important implications for the design and implementation of online learning platforms.

生成式人工智能如何影响元认知与自我调节学习：促进还是抑制？ How GenAI Affects Metacognition and Self-Regulated Learning: Enhancement or Inhibition? 第一作者 First Author

李子健, 樊一舟

Zijian Li, Yizhou Fan

Routledge 图书章节, 2024 (已接收) In: Routledge Book Chapter, 2024 (Accepted)

本章探讨了生成式人工智能（GenAI）对学习者元认知和自我调节学习的影响。我们分析了GenAI技术在教育中的应用，讨论了其对学习过程的促进作用和潜在的抑制效应。研究为教育工作者如何有效利用GenAI工具提供了理论框架和实践建议。

This chapter explores the impact of Generative AI (GenAI) on learners' metacognition and self-regulated learning. We analyze the application of GenAI technology in education and discuss both its enhancement effects and potential inhibition on the learning process. The study provides a theoretical framework and practical recommendations for educators on how to effectively utilize GenAI tools.

本文探讨了大型语言模型（LLM）在教育领域的应用及其影响。我们分析了LLM在个性化学习、智能辅导、自动评估等方面的潜力，并讨论了实施过程中面临的挑战和机遇。研究为未来的教育技术发展提供了新的视角。
